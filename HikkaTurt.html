About Hikkaduwa! and Why?

Hikkaduwa is a charming coastal town located on the south coast of Sri Lanka that has become a popular destination around the world. The town is known for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and abundant marine life, making it a perfect place for those looking for a mix of relaxation and adventure. The clear blue waters of Hikkaduwa are ideal for swimming, snorkeling, and scuba diving, providing visitors with the opportunity to see a variety of colorful fish, turtles, and other marine creatures. The town also boasts a thriving nightlife scene, with numerous bars and clubs offering live music and entertainment. Hikkaduwa is also home to a variety of restaurants and cafes, serving delicious Sri Lankan cuisine as well as international fare. For those looking to explore the surrounding area, there are plenty of activities available, such as visiting the nearby Galle Fort, taking a boat ride on the Madu River, or visiting a nearby turtle hatchery. Overall, Hikkaduwa offers a unique and exciting experience for tourists, combining beautiful beaches, vibrant culture, and stunning marine life.


Turtle Hatcheries in Hikkaduwa

The hatcheries offer a range of educational programs and guided tours, allowing visitors to witness the hatching of baby turtles and even participate in the release of these turtles back into the ocean. The hatcheries are an excellent opportunity for tourists to learn about the conservation efforts being made to protect the sea turtle population and the importance of preserving Sri Lanka's marine ecosystems. By visiting these hatcheries, tourists can not only enjoy a unique and educational experience, but also contribute to the conservation efforts that are vital to protecting the sea turtles in Hikkaduwa and beyond.

Visiting the turtle hatchery in Hikkaduwa, Sri Lanka is a wonderful way to make a difference and contribute to the conservation efforts that are essential for the protection of sea turtles. The hatchery is home to several species of turtles, including the endangered green turtle, and works tirelessly to protect these creatures from poaching and other threats. By visiting the hatchery, you can learn about the important work being done to protect the turtles and even participate in the release of baby turtles back into the ocean. This hands-on experience is a fantastic opportunity to witness the wonder of nature up close and make a meaningful contribution to conservation efforts. By supporting the hatchery through your visit, you are helping to ensure that these magnificent creatures continue to thrive for generations to come. So, why not visit the turtle hatchery in Hikkaduwa and make a difference? Your visit can help protect these beautiful creatures and preserve the unique marine ecosystem of Sri Lanka
